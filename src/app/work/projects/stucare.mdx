## Overview

A management application for educational care centers to coordinate schedules, attendance, and parent communication. It bridges the gap between childcare administrators and busy parents.

## Key Features

- **Attendance Tracking**: Real-time entry log for student arrivals and departures.
- **Parent Portal**: Direct messaging, daily logs, and activity reports for families.
- **Administrative Tools**: Staff schedules and student roster organization.
