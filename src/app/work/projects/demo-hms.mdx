## Overview

A comprehensive clinic and hospital administration portal built to streamline scheduling, patient records, and billing workflows. Designed to improve patient experience and operational efficiency for modern healthcare facilities.

## Key Features

- **Patient & Appointment Management**: Simplified booking system for patients and clinic staff.
- **Medical Billing**: Automated invoicing, prescriptions, and billing generation.
- **Electronic Health Records (EHR)**: Secure digital repository for storing patient health summaries and histories.
